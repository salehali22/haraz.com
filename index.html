Origin Of Yemeni Coffee

Farmers like to call the fruits of Haraz Red, The Red Agate (Al Aqeeq Al Ahmar) , compared to the precious and famous Yemeni stone to express their appreciation for these fruits. Haraz region see's an altitude of 1800-2460 MASL. The temperature is moderate and humidity is low. The only source of water to irrigate the plants is 100% from rain and fresh spring waters that come out of the high-rise mountains All coffee trees are organic, though not certified, and are harvested fully by hand when the fruits are mature and have reached the Haraz Red color. This slow growth ensures dynamic flavor profiles that range in complexities thanks to old growth plantations dating back many hundreds of years ago.. Everything is dried on raised beds and carefully turned by hand so that the surface of the shell receives equal amount of sunlight, until the internal moisture reaches 11-13%.
